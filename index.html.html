Why Digital Consent Matters

At NaCOS, we are committed to transparency and protecting your personal information. This system allows you to:

Grant Consent Easily

Review and provide informed consent for specific events and data collection activities from anywhere.

Manage Your Preferences

You have control. View and update your consent choices for photography, data usage, and research participation at any time.

Ensure Compliance

Helps the department adhere to data protection regulations and ethical guidelines set by the university.

How The DCMS Works

A simple, secure, and transparent process for managing consent.

1

Administrator Creates Form

An admin creates a digital consent form for a specific event or purpose.

2

Student Receives Notification

Targeted students receive a request to review and provide consent.

3

Student Grants Consent

The student logs in, reviews the terms, and grants or denies consent digitally.

4

Records are Securely Archived

All responses are timestamped and stored for compliance and future reference.
